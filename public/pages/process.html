From Waste to Wear

1 pair of EcoSlides for every 1 transformed tire.

Before

01 Collect & Select

We collect discarded tires and choose strong rubber sections suitable for upcycling into durable soles.

  • Careful selection for strength and longevity.
  • Local sourcing and responsible reuse.

02 Clean & Inspect

We clean the rubber and inspect it to ensure quality and safety.

What you get: clean rubber ready for cutting.

03 Cut Sidewall

We remove sidewalls to access workable, strong rubber sections.

What you get: usable rubber sheets.

04 Mark Template

We trace the sandal outline for consistent sizing and fit.

What you get: precise sole outlines.

05 Cut & Shape Soles

We cut the rubber and refine the shape by hand.

What you get: formed tire-rubber soles.

06 Sand & Smooth

We sand edges and surfaces for comfort, finish, and durability.

What you get: smooth, safe edges.

07 Prepare Straps

Straps are cut and finished for strength and comfort.

What you get: hand-finished straps.

08 Punch & Fit

We punch holes and test strap placement for a secure fit.

What you get: ready-to-assemble parts.

09 Assemble

We assemble each pair by hand and fine-tune the fit.

What you get: assembled EcoSlides.

10 Final Check

We check comfort, durability, and finish—so every pair is ready to wear and made to last.

What you get: comfortable, long-lasting sandals.
