From Waste to Wear

1 pair of EcoSlides for every 1 transformed tire.

Step 01

01 Collect & Select

We collect discarded tires and choose strong rubber sections suitable for upcycling into durable soles.

  • Careful selection for strength and longevity.
  • Local sourcing and responsible reuse.

02 Select Tires

We choose the best tire sections for durability and safety.

03 Cut Sidewall

We remove sidewalls to access workable, strong rubber sections.

04 Mark Template

We trace the sandal outline for consistent sizing and fit.

05 Template Check

We validate the shape and sizing before cutting.

06 Sand & Smooth

We sand edges and surfaces for comfort and durability.

07 Adjust Straps

We prepare and adjust straps for comfort and strength.

08 Hammer & Fit

We secure components and test strap placement.

09 Assemble

We assemble each pair by hand and refine the fit.

Step 10

10 Final Check

We check comfort, durability, and finish—so every pair is ready to wear and made to last.
