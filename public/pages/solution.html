01 Collect & Select

We collect discarded tires and choose strong rubber sections suitable for upcycling into durable soles.

  • Careful selection for strength and longevity.
  • Local sourcing and responsible reuse.

02 Select Tires

We choose the best tire sections for durability and safety.

03 Cut Sidewall

We remove sidewalls to access workable, strong rubber sections.

04 Mark Template

We trace the sandal outline for consistent sizing and fit.

05 Template Check

We validate the shape and sizing before cutting.

06 Sand & Smooth

We sand edges and surfaces for comfort and durability.

07 Adjust Straps

We prepare and adjust straps for comfort and strength.

08 Hammer & Fit

We secure components and test strap placement.

09 Assemble

We assemble each pair by hand and refine the fit.

Step 10

10 Final Check

Prototype (Origin)

EcoSlides started with a simple experiment and a strong belief: waste doesn't have to stay waste.

  • This first prototype was built to test an idea transforming tire strips into functional footwear that could withstand daily use.
  • Stability, structure, and durability came first.
  • The goal wasn't perfection, but proof: proof that discarded materials could be reshaped, trusted, and worn with confidence.

Support Fit (Origin Prototype)

Our starting point. Built to feel stable and durable — then refined into later models with better comfort and fit.

  • Recycled straps
  • Stable feel
  • Prototype foundation

Evolution

After our first prototype, we refined the designs into everyday-ready models improving hold, comfort, and versatility while keeping the same mission: upcycled tire strips with real durability.

Classic Cross-Strap

Our secure everyday build. The crossed tire-strip straps distribute pressure evenly, improving hold and comfort for daily wear.

Urban Strap

Easy on, easy off. Two tire-rubber straps deliver a clean look and dependable grip, made for quick everyday movement.

Trail Sandal

Minimal and rugged. A simpler strap with tire-rubber traction — made for outdoor days and rougher surfaces.

Panama Heritage

A tribute to tradition — reimagined with tire strips. This is where local identity meets circular design.

La Cutarra (Panama Edition)

A cultural classic reimagined with tire strips to honor tradition while reducing waste.

  • Tradition: A timeless Panamanian silhouette.
  • Craft: Hand-finished tire-strip weaving.
  • Impact: Waste-to-wear design with purpose.
