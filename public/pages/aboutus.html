OUR STORY

From a local problem to a practical solution

EcoSlides began with one question: why do so many tires become waste when the material is built to last?

In many communities, discarded tires pile up, get burned, or end up in nature creating pollution and long-term damage. Tire waste is tough, bulky, and slow to break down.

We wanted to build something practical and affordable: footwear that gives rubber a second life, while supporting local craftsmanship and responsible production.

Today, EcoSlides prototypes evolve through hands-on testing and continuous improvement focused on comfort, durability, and low-impact materials.
